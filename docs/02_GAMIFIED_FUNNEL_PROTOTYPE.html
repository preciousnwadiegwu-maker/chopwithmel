Stage 1 · Acquisition

The Meta ad — hook with the game, not the food

A scroll-stopping Instagram/Facebook ad leads with the game and the reward ("Spin to win up to 40% off"), not a generic menu shot. The prize is the hook; the food is the payoff. Food is Meta's cheapest, highest-converting vertical — and Nigerian clicks are remarkably cheap.

Stage 2 · Capture & Convert

The ChopWheel — every spin wins, every spinner converts

Instead of a flat "10% off" box, visitors spin a wheel and "earn" their discount. Earned rewards feel more valuable and redeem far more often. Capture is a single WhatsApp field — the lowest-friction, highest-trust channel in Lagos.

Stage 4 · Retention & Virality

The loyalty loop — turn one order into a habit + a referral

Three compounding mechanics fire after order #1: a time-boxed streak ("order 3× in 14 days → reward"), points & tiers, and a double-sided "give ₦1,000 / get ₦1,000" referral. This is where the real money is — it attacks your 20% churn and feeds cheap referred customers back into the top.
